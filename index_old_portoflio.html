Alejandro Garcia

Engineering - Robotics - Analytics



Alejandro Garcia is a Visiting Student at the City Science group. He recently graduated in Industrial Engineering from Tecnun-University of Navarra in Spain and is currently working towards his engineering master thesis at the MIT Media Lab City Science Group


My learning path is driven by an special interest in R&D and entrepreneurship, focusing on the development of new technologies as the main inspiratoin to have an impact on our current society. Currently interested in data analytics as a powerful tool to understand and drive a positive change.
