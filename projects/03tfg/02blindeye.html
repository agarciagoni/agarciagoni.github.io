Home Data - Piccolo Kitchen

The project consists of a transformable kitchen, using architectural robotics for a hyper-efficent use of space. To make it more interesring, in a built prototype we include a wide range of sensors aiming to detect activity and study behavior in a new transformable enviroment. More info about the complete proyect here! .

The project consits of a network of simple, cheap, noninvasive sensors to minimize the privacy invasion. Through non personal data such as temperature, humidity, sound levels, distance from the cooktop or cabient interactions the goal is to study the behavior of user in transformable enviroments. The final objective is to build a human activity recognition system (HAR) through non invasive sensors.

The sensor included in the protype are the following:
  • Ultrasonic Sensor Y401
  • Matbotix Ultrasonic range finder EZ
  • VL53L0X distance_sonic sensor
  • Sound sensor SEN-12642 x2
  • PIR sensor x3
  • Buttons x7 ( 3 backside,2 upper cabinet left, 2 upper cabinet right)
  • TerMITes x1 (Measuring Temperature, Humidity and Light)
